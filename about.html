About

We are based out of Waltham, Massachusetts. The inspiration to create this application came from working directly with Special Needs students in a school setting for over 5 years. Being in the classroom, as well as working with some amazing Behavior Specialists granted us the understanding necessary to design from multiple perspectives, with different user needs in mind.

The product is never complete, and we are always looking for more features and feedback. Please reach out with any questions, comments, and especially suggestions. Don't forget to spread the word, and tell other Teachers, School Systems and Specialists about us!

Education

The task of effectively recording education data is complicated. Many different schools and teachers use different techniques to record data, so for the mean time, we took the simple approach of recording only accuracy and independence, with the hope of expanding in the future. Suggestions are welcome.

Features

With the proper time (and funding) we hope to add many features, such as medication/food tracking, classroom management, school wide support, and others. Let us know if there are features you feel should be top priority.

Funding / Investors

If you or someone you know is interested in helping us reach our goals and design a great product, please don't hesitate to get in touch. Your support is greatly appreciated.
