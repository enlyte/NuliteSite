About

We are based out of Waltham, Massachusetts. The inspiration to create this application came from working directly with Special Needs students in a school setting for over 5 years. Learning from amazing Behavior Specialists, and working hand in hand (and sometimes hand over hand) with students in the classroom has granted us the understanding necessary to design from multiple perspectives, with different user needs in mind. The product is never complete, and we are always looking for more features and feedback. Please reach out with any questions, comments, and especially suggestions.
