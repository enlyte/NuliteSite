About VidCut AI

VidCut AI is designed to automate the video editing process for podcasts, focusing on enhancing the visual experience for viewers. Our advanced AI technology ensures that the active speaker is always highlighted, providing a dynamic and engaging viewing experience.

Turn a single video clip into a fully edited podcast with multiple angles!

What is VidCut AI?

VidCut AI is a powerful desktop application available for both Mac and Windows. It allows you to easily select and process your video files, leveraging advanced AI to automate the editing process. Once your videos are processed, you can export them as a Final Cut Pro (FCP) project, with compatibility for more video editing software coming soon.

  • Select and process video files with ease
  • Automate video editing using advanced AI technology
  • Export projects in FCP format, with more formats to come
  • Advanced audio to transcription features Learn More

Current Features & Capabilities

Unlike other products, VidCut AI only requires one audio track with ALL speakers to create accurate camera cuts, making it more accessible and user-friendly

For multi-camera setups, VidCut AI seamlessly integrates into Final Cut Pro, allowing you to add and switch between different camera angles

How to Process a Video

  1. Step 1 (Optional): Organize your footage into a folder with identifiable filenames
  2. Step 2: Start a new VidCut project and select video files to add
  3. Step 3: Press a button for pre-processing to extract audio
  4. Step 4: Press a button to send the extracted audio to AI processing
  5. Step 5: Export the project file (currently supporting Final Cut Pro)
  6. Step 6: Open the project file in FCP, make any additional edits, and export the final video
