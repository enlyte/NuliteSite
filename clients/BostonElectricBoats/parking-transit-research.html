Budget Tips

  • Pre-book online via SpotHero, ParkWhiz, or BestParking for discounted rates (often 30-50% off walk-up prices).
  • Early bird specials at Dock Square Garage and Boston Harbor Garage can save significantly.
  • Sargent's Wharf and the Lewis Wharf lot are the closest options and often cheapest for a full day.

2. Public Transit (MBTA / "The T")

Nearest Subway Station: Aquarium (Blue Line)

  • Line: Blue Line
  • Address: State Street at Atlantic Avenue (under the surface)
  • Walking distance to Lewis Wharf: Approximately 0.4 miles / 5-7 minute walk north along Atlantic Avenue
  • Walking directions:

1. Exit Aquarium station onto Atlantic Avenue

2. Turn right (north) and walk along Atlantic Avenue

3. Pass Long Wharf and Commercial Wharf on your right

4. Lewis Wharf / 28 Atlantic Ave will be on your right

  • Connections: The Blue Line connects to the Orange Line at State Street station and the Green Line at Government Center (both one stop away)

Alternative Station: Haymarket (Orange Line + Green Line)

  • Lines: Orange Line, Green Line
  • Address: New Sudbury Street at Congress Street
  • Walking distance to Lewis Wharf: Approximately 0.5 miles / 8-10 minute walk
  • Walking directions:

1. Exit Haymarket station

2. Walk east on New Sudbury Street toward the waterfront

3. Turn right (south) on Atlantic Avenue

4. Lewis Wharf / 28 Atlantic Ave will be on your left

  • Bonus: Haymarket station is right next to the famous Haymarket open-air produce market (Fri/Sat) and Faneuil Hall / Quincy Market

Bus Service

  • Nearest bus stop: Atlantic Ave @ Commercial Wharf (MBTA stop #30235) -- essentially at Lewis Wharf
  • Useful routes: Several bus routes run along Atlantic Avenue
  • SL4 (Silver Line): Runs along the waterfront corridor

Commuter Rail

  • North Station is approximately 0.7 miles / 12-15 minute walk from Lewis Wharf
  • Serves the Fitchburg, Haverhill, Lowell, and Newburyport/Rockport commuter rail lines
  • Walking: Head west on Commercial St, then north on Causeway St

Ferry

  • Long Wharf (a few wharves south) has ferry service to Charlestown Navy Yard, the Harbor Islands, and other destinations

3. Street Parking

Metered Parking on Atlantic Avenue

  • Location: Meters exist along Atlantic Avenue between The Sail Loft restaurant and Sargent's Wharf (directly in the Lewis Wharf area)
  • Time limit: 2 hours maximum
  • Active hours: Monday-Saturday, 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM
  • Rate: Standard Boston meter rate (~$1.25/hr, varies by zone)
  • Payment: Coins, credit/debit cards, or the ParkBoston app
  • CRITICAL: These meters convert to resident-only parking from 6:00 PM to 8:00 AM. You will be ticketed/towed if parked during resident hours without a permit.

Sundays & Holidays

  • All Boston parking meters are FREE on Sundays -- no time limits apply
  • Meters are also free on major holidays

Other Nearby Metered Streets

  • North Washington Street (between Cooper St and Charlestown Bridge): Meters here are open to anyone 24/7, making them more flexible for evening/night use
  • Commercial Street: Some metered spots, check posted signs

Street Parking Tips

1. Arrive early -- street spots near the waterfront fill up fast, especially in summer

2. Read the signs carefully -- many spots switch between meter and resident-only at different times

3. Download the ParkBoston app before arriving to avoid fumbling with the meter

4. 2-hour limit makes street parking impractical for longer boat rentals -- use a garage instead

5. Summer weekends are the hardest time to find street parking in the North End waterfront area

6. Street cleaning: Watch for posted street cleaning schedules, which temporarily prohibit parking
