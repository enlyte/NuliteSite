Good to Know

Parking & Directions

Depart from Lewis Wharf, 28 Atlantic Ave, Boston. Metered street parking available on Atlantic Ave and Commercial St. Nearby garages at Sargent's Wharf and Parcel 7. T-accessible via Aquarium station (Blue Line).

What to Bring

Sunscreen, sunglasses, an extra layer (it's cooler on the water), drinks and snacks (BYOB!), and a Bluetooth playlist for the speakers. We provide life jackets and everything else.

Weather Policy

Boats operate rain or shine. Canopy covers are available on all boats. In the event of severe weather (lightning, high winds), we'll contact you to reschedule at no charge.

Cancellation

Full refund if cancelled 48+ hours before your cruise. Within 48 hours, you can reschedule to another available date at no cost. No-shows are non-refundable.
